Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

To process your participation in the World Talent Olympiad, we collect the following personal data via WhatsApp and our forms:

  • Participant Details: Full name, age, and location.
  • Guardian Details (if applicable): Parent or legal guardian's name and contact number.
  • Media Data: The talent video submission and any photos provided for social media posters.
  • Contact Information: WhatsApp number and email address (for certificate delivery).
  • Payment Details: Bank account or UPI ID (strictly for the top 3 winners during the promotional phase to transfer cash rewards).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To process your ₹199 entry fee.
  • To review and evaluate the talent submission.
  • To issue and deliver the digital Certificate of Appreciation.
  • To design and publish promotional posters of the Top 10 participants on our social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, etc.).
  • To process cash reward payouts to winners.
  • To communicate updates regarding the competition via WhatsApp.

3. Children’s Privacy (DPDP Act Compliance)

We are committed to protecting the privacy of minors. We do not process the personal data of anyone under the age of 18 without verifiable consent from a parent or legal guardian. We do not use children's data for targeted behavioral advertising or track them across third-party platforms.

4. Data Retention and Deletion (7-Day Deletion Policy)

We respect your privacy and do not permanently store your media files. We only hold your submitted talent video for the time necessary to review it for the competition. Within seven (7) days of announcing the final results for that competition cycle, all submitted talent videos will be permanently deleted from our databases and local storage. We do not retain, archive, or build a database of user video files after the competition concludes. Posters and PR materials created for the Top 10 winners will remain on our public social media feeds as per the granted media rights, but the original video files will be destroyed.

5. Data Sharing and Security

  • Third Parties: We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with trusted service providers (e.g., payment gateways for the entry fee and reward distribution, or graphic designers for creating posters) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Public Display: As part of the competition, the names and likenesses of the Top 10 participants will be made public on our social media channels.
  • Security: We implement reasonable security practices to protect your data from unauthorized access. However, no digital platform is 100% secure.

6. Your Rights

Under India’s Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request the correction of inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time (which will result in the removal of your video from our competition and social media, though the ₹199 entry fee remains non-refundable).
  • Request the deletion of your data once the competition cycle concludes.
